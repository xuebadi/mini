Tinyverse

Own a world, build it your way, publish it to the tinyverse, and earn from every harvest.

From empty land to living world

Three steps from unclaimed island to a world with real players.

  1. 1

    Claim a world

    Browse the tinyverse map and buy an unclaimed world slug with USDC on Solana. Your name goes on the deed.

  2. 2

    Build it

    Open your world in the editor. Shape terrain, place resources like water and stone, add crops and animals, then save your draft.

  3. 3

    Publish and earn

    Set your tax rate (1–100%) and publish. Players enter your world, harvest resources, and your cut arrives automatically.

What you get

A live, authoritative game room backed by your world data.

Real-time multiplayer

Up to dozens of players share the same live room via PartyKit. Every move, harvest, and chat message syncs instantly.

Avatars

Players pick from five avatar classes — Knight, Baird, Wizard, Knave, or Template. Their choice persists across sessions.

Tax economy

Every harvest splits the reward between the player and you (the world owner). You set the percentage — idle income from every session.

Live chat

Players chat inside your world in real time. Messages are server-ordered and server-stamped — no spoofing or replay.

Four harvestable resources

Fish from water, ore from stone, plants from crops, and meat from animals. Each node regenerates on its own timer.

Design your resource map

The tiles you place in the builder determine what players can harvest.

Water tiles

Connected water bodies produce one fish node. The larger the body, the more total charges available before regen.

Stone tiles

Each stone cell is an independent ore node with a randomised tier (1–3 charges). Ore respawns on a timer.

Crop and plant tiles

Crop, corn, wheat, pumpkin, carrot, sunflower tiles each produce a gatherable plant node that ripens after harvest.

Animals

Cow and sheep tiles seed the world with huntable animals. Animals respawn automatically to keep the world populated.
