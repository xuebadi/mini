Code of Conduct

Last updated: June 2026

TinyWorld is for building, playing, sharing, and helping each other. Keep the community safe, constructive, and welcoming.

Zero tolerance: policy violations immediately suspend both community and game access: first violation 4 hours, second violation 24 hours, third violation permanent ban.

Do

  • Be friendly, constructive, and helpful.
  • Share TinyWorld builds, ideas, and feedback in good faith.
  • Use reports/downvotes when something looks unsafe or against the rules.
  • Respect moderators and automated safety systems.

Do not

  • Sell, promote, shill, or discuss other contract addresses, meme coins, tokens, presales, airdrops, or financial schemes.
  • Use abuse, harassment, threats, hate, slurs, foul language, or targeted negativity.
  • Post sexual, nude, explicit, exploitative, or unsafe content.
  • Spam, raid, impersonate, evade bans, or try to bypass rate limits/moderation.
  • Use the community to manipulate, scam, mislead, or pressure other users.

Enforcement

Violations are enforced immediately. Moderation may be automated, human-reviewed, or both. A detected violation can remove the message and apply the current strike penalty to both chat/community features and game access.

Reporting

Use downvote/report controls on posts to self-report problem content. Reports feed moderation workflows and may be reviewed by moderators or automation.
